About Online_Exam

Online exams, also known as e-exams, use the internet to conduct assessments. They offer flexibility, allowing students to take tests from home or a designated exam center. This can reduce stress and save time, while also benefiting teachers by streamlining the exam process. Online exams also have environmental advantages by reducing paper usage and logistics.
